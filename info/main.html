Introduction

Welcome to the website of Transportation Study in Department of Geography. This website is operated by Department of Geography, The Ohio State University. We focus on geography of transportation, such as accessibility analysis, spatial interaction, hub-and-spoke network and other topics.

Current Project

Sky Explorer - T100
Launch the app • Introduction

This WebGIS provides an interactive way to explore T100 data provided by BTS. User can investigate the passenger and freight flows between two airports, the flow trend of an airport, the load factor of a route, and even the operating aircrafts in each route by airline.

Air Accessibility Map

This project provides an overview of current US domestic air passenger traffic. It shows the results in detailed maps of the air service accessibility of individual airports (places).
