About United Kingdom CAA Data

UK Civil Aviation Authority is the statutory corporation which oversees and regulates all aspects of civil aviation in the United Kingdom. It provides brief monthly statistics of passenger flights on domestic and international routes. This data is aggregated by inbound and outbouund flow.
