About Taiwan CAA Data

The Civil Aeronautics Administration, Ministry of Transportation and Communication is a government agency of the Ministry of Transportation and Communications of the Republic of China which is responsible for the regulation of all civil aviation activities in Taiwan. It releases monthly and annual report with passenger aviation statistics.
