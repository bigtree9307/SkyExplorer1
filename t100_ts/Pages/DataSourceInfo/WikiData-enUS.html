About Wikipedia Airport Information Page

Wikipedia provides detailed introduction of most civil airports all over the world. In these pages, a table shows available destinations with operating airlines. Although this data is not as accurate as the official time table released by airlines, it is still a trustable resource of available connections of an airport.

All the airport data is obtained from Wikipedia English version.
